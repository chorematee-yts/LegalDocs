## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use ChoreMate, we collect the following information that you provide to us:

**Profile Information:**
- **Name:** Your full name or display name
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation, login, and communication
- **Profile Image:** An optional photo you upload to personalize your account
- **Location:** Your general location (city/region) to customize your experience

**Household and Task Information:**
- Family member names and profiles
- Chore assignments and task descriptions
- Completion status and history
- Rewards and points tracking
- Any notes or comments you add to tasks

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage and Analytics Data:**

We automatically collect certain information about how you use the App, including:

- Device information (device type, operating system version, device identifiers)
- App usage statistics (features used, time spent in app, frequency of use)
- Performance data (app crashes, loading times, errors)
- Interaction data (buttons clicked, screens viewed, navigation patterns)
- IP address and general location data
- Date and time of app access

**Technical Information:**
- Mobile operating system (Android/iOS version)
- App version you're using
- Language preferences
- Time zone settings

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

If you choose to sign in using third-party services (such as Google Sign-In), we may receive:
- Your name
- Email address  
- Profile picture
- Any other information you authorize the third-party service to share
