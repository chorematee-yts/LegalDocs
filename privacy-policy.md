## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

-   Name or display name
-   Email address
-   Optional profile photo
-   Household names and member profiles
-   Tasks, assignments, notes, comments, rewards, and completion history

### Information Collected Automatically

-   Device type and operating system
-   App version
-   Language and time zone
-   Crash reports and diagnostics
-   Anonymous analytics and performance information

### Third-Party Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we receive the information you authorize
Google to share, such as your name, email address, and profile photo.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: - Provide and maintain ChoreMate -
Synchronize your household across devices - Send task reminders and
notifications (if enabled) - Improve reliability, performance, and
features - Respond to support requests - Protect users and prevent
abuse - Comply with legal obligations

We do **not sell** your personal information.

We may share information:

-   With members of your household as required for the App to function.

-   During a merger, acquisition, or business transfer.

No online service can guarantee absolute security.
